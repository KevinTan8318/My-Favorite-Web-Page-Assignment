1. Basketball

What basketball is: You are given a basketball (a type of ball) and you throw the ball into the net. Typically you are able to play basketball alone or with others. If you play alone, you can just shoot for fun. In a regular basketball game two teams with 5 players each compete. The goal is to score the most amount of points in a specific amount of time to win. Whoever reaches the highest points by the end is crowned the winning team.

Why I like basketball

  • I started playing while I was a child at the age of 9
  • Lots of my friends play basketball
  • Its fun to learn new moves and see yourself improve/get better over time
  • Its a good way for me get exercise in
  • My dad got me into basketball because he used to play
  • I like being competitive

2. My Favourite Video Game

My favourite game to play is Roblox. Roblox is a player generated game where people who play the game are able to create games and play others games. So basically you are able to play games made by others or you can make your own game to share with others. Roblox is my favourite game because of the endless possibilities with little to no limit. There are millions of games to choose from so you are not limited into placing a select few. You are able to decide on what to play by looking at the millions of games available.

Why Roblox Is my favourite Video Game

  • There are millions of games to choose from
  • You are able to create your own games
  • You can interact with people all around the world and make new friends
  • When creating a game, you are able to learn new skills
  • Learning Lua (the coding language for Roblox) is fun when you are able to complete something
